# Reusable Frontend Prompts

Use these prompts when starting future websites. They are designed to fight the default AI-generated SaaS template look.

## 1. Brand-Grade Company Website

Build a professional brand-grade company website, not a generic SaaS landing page.

Style references: Apple, OpenAI, Anthropic, Vercel, Linear, MotionSites-style premium hero prompts, and the Agentrix-style immersive AI website. Use the references for taste and structure, not direct copying.

Hard constraints:

- Do not use repeated feature-card grids.
- Do not use the default hero + 3 cards + CTA layout.
- Do not use many rounded glass cards.
- Do not use dashboard mockups unless they are central to the product.
- Do not make every section an eyebrow + heading + cards.
- Use at most 5 homepage sections.

Homepage structure:

1. Immersive first viewport with one short headline and one strong visual.
2. Architecture/worldview section that explains the system with a large visual or linear structure.
3. Product/service map with 3-4 major offerings, displayed as large editorial columns or bands.
4. One credibility/proof section.
5. Minimal CTA.

Design direction:

- Large typography.
- Minimal chrome.
- Thin dividers instead of cards.
- Strong spacing rhythm.
- Few but confident words.
- Visual hierarchy over decoration.

## 2. AI Company Website

Create an AI company website with a serious, premium, future-facing feel.

Avoid:

- Neon cyberpunk style.
- Purple gradient overload.
- Floating glass cards.
- Generic AI buzzword blocks.
- Too many feature bullets.

Use:

- Immersive hero.
- One conceptual visual system.
- Architecture narrative.
- Product map.
- Calm technical confidence.
- Clear customer-facing copy.

Copy style:

- Short, direct, external-facing.
- No internal strategy language.
- No academic framing unless the page is specifically about research.

Example tone:

- "AI-native systems for water operations."
- "Forecast, review, and act from one operating layer."
- "Built for teams that need decisions, not another dashboard."

## 3. Minimal Corporate Website

Build a clean corporate website with strong editorial layout.

Use:

- White, black, or restrained dark theme.
- Large headings.
- Text-led hierarchy.
- Thin rules.
- No decorative cards.
- No excessive illustrations.

Homepage:

- Hero statement.
- Company positioning.
- Capabilities as rows.
- Selected proof.
- Contact CTA.

Design rule:

If a section can be represented as rows, use rows instead of cards.

## 4. Product Website Without SaaS Template Smell

Build a product website without using the common SaaS template formula.

Avoid:

- Hero with floating dashboard screenshots.
- Feature card grid.
- Pricing-like cards for non-pricing content.
- Large rounded containers around every section.

Use:

- Product hero with one real product scene.
- Workflow narrative.
- One strong product architecture diagram.
- Product capabilities as sequenced sections.
- Customer outcomes as concise rows.

The product should feel real, not like a template.

## 5. Prompt Add-On: Anti-Card Rewrite

Rewrite this page to remove the card-heavy template look.

Rules:

- Reduce card count by at least 70%.
- Convert conceptual cards into rows, bands, split sections, or diagrams.
- Keep only cards that represent actual separate objects.
- Increase headline confidence.
- Reduce body copy.
- Remove decorative effects that do not clarify the product.
- Make the first viewport feel like a brand moment.

Output:

- Updated component structure.
- Updated CSS.
- Explanation of what was removed and why.

## 6. HydroAgent-Lab House Style Prompt

Build a HydroAgent-Lab page using the house style in `HYDROAGENT_VISUAL_LANGUAGE.md`.

Design direction:

- Dark editorial, not generic SaaS.
- Cinematic hydrologic operations imagery.
- Full-width fixed navigation.
- Wide layout, no narrow shell.
- Large confident typography.
- Thin dividers and rows instead of card grids.
- Restrained cyan / green-blue accent.
- No purple AI-gradient identity.
- No decorative blobs, floating cards, or tiny labels.

Hero:

- Full-bleed image or cinematic scene.
- Large left-aligned headline.
- Short supporting copy.
- One primary CTA and one secondary CTA.
- No delayed layer reveal or visible loading flash.

Content:

- Customer-facing statements only.
- HydroAgent-Lab is the umbrella brand.
- HydroAgent-FF is one flagship product, not the whole company.
- Show products, workflow systems, expert services, research collaboration, and team credibility.
